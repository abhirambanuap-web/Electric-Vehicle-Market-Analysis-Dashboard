Dashboard Summary (Analysis)

The Electric Vehicle Data Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of electric vehicle adoption, manufacturer performance, and geographic distribution. The dashboard analyzes 114,595 total vehicles, divided into Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV).

Key Insights

Total Vehicles

Total EVs analyzed: 114,595

Vehicle Type Distribution

BEV: 87,767 vehicles (76.59%)

PHEV: 26,828 vehicles (23.41%)

This shows that fully electric vehicles dominate the market, indicating a shift toward zero-emission transportation.

Top Manufacturers
The dashboard highlights the Top 10 EV manufacturers by number of vehicles.

Key leaders include:

Tesla – 52,672 vehicles (market leader)

Nissan – 12,839 vehicles

Chevrolet – 10,273 vehicles

Ford, BMW, Kia, Toyota follow with moderate shares.

Tesla clearly dominates the EV market in this dataset.
